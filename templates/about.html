Our Mission

EvBagel is dedicated to demonstrating best practices in Go web development. We showcase how to effectively combine traditional HTML templates with modern component-based architecture using Templ.

Our approach allows developers to leverage the simplicity of Go's standard library while enjoying the benefits of reusable, type-safe components where they make sense.
